FAMBO FILM FOUNDATION

With the covid-19 pandemic, alot of people in Kibra constituency have lost jobs, contracts, and income. The Fambo Film Foundation, is a project that I am working on to voice the untold and the unheard stories of the residents. There is over 500,000 people most of them are low income, daily wage earners.Since the directives to flatten the curve of the spread of the coronavirus disease, it has made the situation for the dwellers much harder. These families include single parented houdeholds, orphaned or child-headed households and households with people living with disabilities within the informal settlement.
